Industrial Design






Problem:

Create a dynamic product by merging minimum 5 office objects into one element.

Solution:

Office Puzzle is an organic wood kit with scissors, stapler, stapler remover, letter opener, memo clip and a USB that can be accommodate in a space of 16 x 5.5 x 4.5 cm. Every object can be taken out with a magnet making a unique experience to the users.

Participation:

investigation, drawings, bain storming, rendering, logo and prototyping.























































Problem:

Invent an element for hair stylists which could improve their job by having their tools always visible, clean and organized.

Solution:

The beauty salon tree cart has different trays at different levels that allows to put and organize just the tools that the stylist will need.

It’s a comfortable decorative and durable furniture made with stainless Steel.

Participation:

Investigation, drawings, brain sotrming, planning, logo and instructive.
